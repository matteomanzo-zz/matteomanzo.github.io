It’s Alive!

It has been a long and winding road, but I’m online!! Probably you’re not so excited as I am but, trust me, working on this blog for 5 weeks, 5 WEEKS, error by error trying to figure out how to set it up properly it hasn’t be easy (especially when your friend next to you does the same exact thing in about 25 minutes without an error..) but anyway…. There are some changes in the air!! First of all, I’m writing a blog! Never done anything like that before!! (I guess I know why now..)
I never understood why people would write a blog, it always sounded creepy to me that all these attention-seeking people who wanted just some feedback are filling pages after pages with they’re holidays adventures or, even worst, tips for a better life.. But I’ve changed my mind.. “Why am I doing it then?” Basically because I want to keep track of the things that I will be doing from now on, stay connected with people, and because it will be fun in two years to read what I am writing now.. Anyhow, apparently writing a blog is not the biggest change that is going on. A few things are happening.. So let’s start with the first one then, because I’m on my way to become a Maker….

PS: As you can see from the subtitle this blog it’s not just about the syntax and so I apologise in advance for all the spelling errors that I’ll do, I’ll try my best and I’m sure you’ll appreciate the effort…
